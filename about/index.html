About

I’m a developer at Wirelab, where I spend most of my time in Vim developing Ruby on Rails apps. We build a lot of promotional games/websites which means the apps we build handle big amounts of users for a short time and then go offline for ever. This means we iterate often so we can implement our newest tricks quickly and we (almost) never have to deal with legacy code, awesome! Besides writing Ruby I make the occasional sidestep to Objective-C, Coffeescript or Actionscript (yeah Flash booooh, but still the best tool for certain tasks).

I’ve spoken at a couple of events about Wirelab, workflow and achieving goals as a company but I have way more to talk about. If you’re curious about one of the topics I’ve written about on this blog please do contact me as I’d love to tell you more.

You can contact me on Twitter and via email.
